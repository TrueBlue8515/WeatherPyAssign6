3 Observable Trends from Weather Data

1)  The closer a city is to the equator (0 latitude) the warmer the temperature is likely to be.
2)  It is easier to recognize trends in the relationship between Temperature and Latitude than it is Temperature and Cloudiness, Wind Speed or Humidity 
3)  The difficulty in recognizing trends between Temperature and Cloudiness, Wind Speed and Humidity may have to do with the large sample size of cities around the globe
